Vishwa Konkani Student Scholarship Fund is looking for 25 bright and talented Konkani Students who aspire to be the Chartered Accountants under its ambitious and innovative CA SUPER 25 Programme.
CA SUPER 25 is a fully sponsored 4 years educational programme, formed by a group of Chartered Accountants under the auspice of World Konkani Centre, to empower talented rural youth from Konkani speaking communities across Karnataka.

SCHOLARSHIP

The ICAI Fees towards CA Intermediate Exam, ICITSS, AICITSS and CA Finals Exam will be completely covered under CA Super 25 Programme. The Scholarship will also cover the cost of Coaching, Mentoring and Fostering under the programme. The total cost of the Scholarship per student will be to the tune of Rs. 4.00 Lakhs.

COACHING

TWO Residential Camps of 3 months each running up to the CA Intermediate and CA Finals examinations. The Camps will have 750 hours of Coaching by experts on subjects under the syllabi. TWO Residential Camps of 3 Days each will be conducted during 2nd and 3rd year to reinforce the concepts. An Orientation Camp of 3 Days will be conducted at the beginning of the CA Super 25 programme to nurture a bond among the participants to enable peer to peer learning.
